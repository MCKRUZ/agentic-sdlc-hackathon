Module 01 — The Agentic Mental Shift
Rethinking the tool

This Is Not Autocomplete

Copilot / Autocomplete
What you've used before
Predicts the next token. Suggests completions inline. Reacts to what you type in the moment. Stateless — it forgets the previous line as soon as you move on. Each suggestion is independent of the last.
Agentic Tool
What this is
Reads your entire codebase. Plans a sequence of steps. Executes — writes files, runs commands, asks questions. Holds context across the whole task from start to finish.
The shift is from  suggestion  →  action
